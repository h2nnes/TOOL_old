INDENT TOOL

Click on a single tile to select it. Click on it again to deselect. Click and drag to select multiple tiles at once in a row. Do the same thing on already selected (black) tiles to deselect all of them at once. Move individual tiles or whole blocks by holding SHIFT and dragging them to the left or the right of the row.
